## Research Summary
My research focuses on applying and developing **spatial omics technologies** to understand the cellular architecture of complex tissues. I have worked extensively with **In Situ Sequencing (ISS)**, Xenium-style data, and multimodal single-cell datasets across human cortex, mouse CNS, and neuroinflammation models.
